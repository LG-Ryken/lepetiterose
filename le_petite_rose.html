Our Story

A Piece of Paris
in Every Bite

"Baking is not just a craft — it is a love letter written in butter and flour."

Le Petite Rose was founded in 1987 by Marguerite Fontaine, a classically trained pastry chef who trained at Le Cordon Bleu in Paris before bringing her craft to London.

Three decades later, her daughter Élise now leads the kitchen, carrying on the family tradition with the same devotion to quality, seasonality, and the quiet magic that transforms simple ingredients into extraordinary pastries.

Every recipe in our repertoire has a story. We believe that a truly great pastry requires patience, precision, and love — and those are the only ingredients we will never compromise on.
