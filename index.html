Our Story

A Piece of Paris
in Every Bite

"Baking is not just a craft — it is a love letter written in butter and flour."

Le Petite Rose was founded in 2024 by Anna, a passionate baker and lover of French pâtisserie who grew up making tarts in her home kitchen before turning that love into something Wellesley could call its own.

Every recipe in our repertoire has a story. We believe that a truly great pastry requires patience, precision, and love — and those are the only ingredients we will never compromise on.
